Tips

Pushback: Carolyn Egelman and collegues found that long review time and excessive review rounds are strong predictors of pushback, which can cause contributors' negative feelings (see their paper).

Interpersonal Conflicts: Naveen Raman and Sophie Qiu found that toxicity score, identity attack, and sentiment can help detect interpersonal conflict (see their papers [1], [2], and [3]).
