I finally found a man who loves me very much.

He othen says to me, "You are the most beautiful girl I've seen. I enjoy looking at you so very much!"

I always reply, "You are deceived, blinded by your love."

He always replies, "Then I'm willing to be deceived for the rest of my life."

However, when I say he's good-looking, he never argues back. I asked him one day, "Are you Narcissus and considering yourself good-looking?"

He says, "No, I'm not. It's simply because whatever you say is correct."

As a researcher, my daily job is to be constantly criticized. He is really my moral support!
